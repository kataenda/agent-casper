# Payout, KYC & Voting Guidance (Notes)

This file provides recommended text and guidance to include in project documentation regarding prize payout, KYC, and community voting. Official rules are governed by DoraHacks — this is advisory content to help participants prepare.

Voting (CSPR.fans)
- DoraHacks uses community voting (CSPR.fans) for selecting the top-3 projects to advance directly to the Final Round.
- Participants should include a short public campaign description and a link to their DoraHacks submission so voters can verify the demo.
- Voting windows and counts are managed by DoraHacks; projects should not attempt vote manipulation. If unsure, contact DoraHacks support.

Payout & KYC (practical checklist)
- Prize payouts are handled by the organizers (Casper Association / DoraHacks). Winners may be required to complete KYC prior to receiving funds.
- Prepare the following information in advance: legal name, wallet address (for USDC or CSPR), preferred payout currency, and any tax-related documentation requested by organizers.
- For teams: designate a single payout recipient and ensure that team agreement covers prize distribution.

Recommended README addition (copyable)

"Prize payout: Winners will be contacted by the organizers. You may be asked to complete standard KYC and provide a payout wallet address. For questions about prizes or payout timelines, contact DoraHacks or Casper Association."

Note: This is guidance only; follow official DoraHacks instructions in the hackathon page and communications for binding rules.
